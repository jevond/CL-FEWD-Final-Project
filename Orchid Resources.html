Orchid Resources

Here you can find some good resources to find more information about orchid care and where to buy orchid supplies. There are NUMEROUS places to find different information, these are just a few sites that I have found to be helpful!

Where can I buy Orchids?

You can find orchids all over the place for sale. The Phal orchids are going to be the ones you find most ofter- they're sold in most grocery/home supply stores and florists. The other species and genus of orchids are a little harder to find. Some of the not so common orchids will be availible at botanical gardens and at orchid society meetings. There is a great orchid show and sale in Louisville every October that is hosted by the Kentucky Orchid Society that is a great place to find the more less common orchids.

You can also find orchids for sale on line. There you can find numerous different species that you wouldn't find in a store. Be careful though - some sellers arent completely honest and you should definitely check out any reviews before committing to purchasing an orchid from an online vendor.
